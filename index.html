About Us

Where space meets emotion.

Studio Five was founded by designer Tal Yehoshua with a vision to redefine the relationship between space and emotion. We specialize in creating unique, custom-tailored concepts driven by a precise, minimalist, and bold aesthetic.

At the core of our practice are the five senses; we approach design not just visually, but as a complete experience — ensuring that everyone who steps into the space feels instinctively that it was thoughtfully crafted for their exact needs. By placing people at the center of everything we do, we always strive to create environments that invite connection and inspire conversation, whether in a public space or a private home.

The Philosophy

Five Senses.
One Studio.

Our name is our philosophy. Every space we design is cross-examined through the lens of the five senses — because that is how we truly experience life.

01SightLight. An architectural manipulation of natural and artificial lighting, designed to subtly transform the space and its atmosphere throughout the day and night.
02TouchLayering textures to create rich, inviting, and warm spaces while maintaining a high, clean aesthetic — this is our expertise.
03HearingThe ability to control spatial acoustics through design, making conscious choices to evoke the exact feeling and energy we wish to create.
04SmellScent is the fastest path to memory, and it is an essential tool in our design process. Every material carries a unique scent that can instantly transport you to another time and place.
05TasteIn its metaphorical sense, our favorite of all. We create spaces defined by the people who occupy them, not the other way around — and that is exactly what makes them so successful.
