Designing with Shadow

July 2026

Light is the most powerful — and most overlooked — material in design. It is never static; it moves through a space across the day, redrawing every surface it meets.

We design the darkness as carefully as the light. A single linear fixture, placed with intent, can turn a flat wall into a living gradient and give a room its rhythm.
